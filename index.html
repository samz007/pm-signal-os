Opportunity intelligence

AI PM point of view

The old PM portfolio was documents. The AI-native one is a working system.

Build toward

Evidence-grounded AI

Outputs should point back to source snippets, assumptions, confidence, and missing evidence so PMs can trust and defend them.

Build toward

Human-in-the-loop agents

The right product is rarely “full autonomy.” It is controlled delegation: agents draft, score, retrieve, and review while humans approve.

Avoid

Prompt-wrapper products

A generic PRD generator does not prove AI product judgment. The product must understand context, constraints, risks, and measurement.

Avoid

AI theater metrics

Adoption, token volume, and clicks are not enough. The bar is customer outcome, workflow compression, and business value.

Recommended build plan

Start with an AI-native Voice of Customer decision engine.

It is the right flagship because it forces the full AI PM muscle: ingestion, context, synthesis, evidence, scoring, evals, governance, and measurable impact.

  1. Signal inbox Import feedback snippets from support, calls, reviews, sales notes, and research docs.
  2. Context layer Attach customer segment, ARR, persona, product area, source, freshness, and confidence.
  3. AI synthesis Cluster jobs, pain, objections, and requests with traceable evidence and missing-data flags.
  4. Eval harness Check citation accuracy, duplicate merging, priority drift, tone, and unsafe unsupported claims.
  5. Decision loop Generate roadmap bets, experiments, risks, stakeholder review, and ROI measurement plan.
