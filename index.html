Build toward

Evidence-to-decision workflows

Avoid as a core

Standalone template generation

PRDs, user stories, meeting notes, and release notes are useful utilities, but they look old when they do not connect to live context, evidence, and risk.

Avoid as a core

Classic MBA frameworks without data

SWOT, BCG, and static market sizing can feel performative. They become stronger only when attached to assumptions, sources, and sensitivity ranges.

Recommended starting point

Start with a Voice of Customer command center.

It is visual, PM-native, defensible, and demoable. It also creates a foundation for PRDs, roadmap bets, experiment ideas, stakeholder reviews, and launch narratives.

  1. Signal inbox Import feedback snippets from support, calls, reviews, sales notes, or CSV.
  2. Theme clustering Group pain points, requests, objections, and jobs-to-be-done with confidence labels.
  3. Opportunity scoring Rank themes by demand, revenue impact, strategic fit, effort, and evidence quality.
  4. Decision packet Generate a concise product brief, risks, next experiment, and stakeholder-ready summary.
  5. Review loop Run customer, engineering, sales, and executive perspectives before the roadmap call.
